How It Works

1

Open the app

See exactly what to do today. 4-6 exercises, sets and reps laid out. No navigation, no choices, no wondering.

2

Do the workout

30-45 minutes. Minimal equipment. Body weight, kettlebell, or barbell. Check off each exercise as you go.

3

You're done

That's it. See what's next. Come back in a day or two. Progress over perfection.
